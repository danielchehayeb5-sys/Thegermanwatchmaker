What We Do

Services

The following are common watch repair and maintenance services — please confirm the full list and pricing directly with the shop.

Mechanical Watch Repair

Diagnosis and repair of mechanical and automatic movements — hands, gears, escapements, and more.

Battery Replacement

Fast, careful battery replacement for quartz watches — done right so the case seal is properly restored.

Watch Cleaning & Servicing

Full ultrasonic cleaning and movement servicing to restore performance and protect your timepiece long-term.

Strap & Bracelet Adjustments

Sizing, link removal, spring bar replacement, and strap fitting for a comfortable, secure fit.

Vintage Watch Care

Sensitive handling of older and vintage timepieces — preserving originality while restoring reliable function.

Water Resistance Checks

Testing and restoration of water resistance ratings — especially important after any case opening or battery change.

About the Shop
